Will

Will is deeply upset by congestion when travelling by the underground. He would like to avoid it but isn’t able to with the apps that he uses. Will would go so far as to double his journey time to avoid the congestion he experiences during his commutes.

“I would rather take twice as long to not be in a completely rammed carriage where you can't even breathe.”
