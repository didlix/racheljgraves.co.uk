Big map of (almost) everything

Project Type: Hackday Prototype
Organisation: NHS Hackdays
Team Size: 5
Role: API Developer

Opportunity

Make health services easier to find.

Results

A prototype for an easy way to publish and find information about health services, places and things that exist in the NHS modern interactive map with health services pulled in from the NHS's healthservices API. Our prototype won a hackday award.

The API would be usable by services such as voice assistants to serve requests like "find my nearest defibrillator".
